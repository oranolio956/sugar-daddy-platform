# Social Features and Community Building

## 1. **User Profiles**
- **Customizable Profiles**: Allow users to customize their profiles with photos, videos, and personal information.
- **Verification Badges**: Provide verification badges for users who have verified their identity.
- **Profile Privacy**: Allow users to control who can see their profile and interact with them.

## 2. **Social Interactions**
- **Likes and Favorites**: Allow users to like and favorite profiles to show interest.
- **Comments and Reactions**: Enable users to comment on and react to posts and photos.
- **Sharing**: Allow users to share profiles, posts, and photos with others.

## 3. **Community Building**
- **Groups**: Enable users to create and join groups based on shared interests.
- **Forums**: Provide forums for users to discuss topics related to sugar dating.
- **Events**: Allow users to create and join events for meeting in person.

## 4. **Content Creation**
- **Blogs**: Allow users to create and share blog posts on topics related to sugar dating.
- **Photos and Videos**: Enable users to upload and share photos and videos.
- **Stories**: Allow users to share temporary stories that disappear after 24 hours.

## 5. **Social Features**
- **Friend Requests**: Allow users to send and accept friend requests.
- **Followers**: Enable users to follow each other to stay updated on their activities.
- **Messaging**: Provide a messaging system for users to communicate with each other.

## 6. **Community Moderation**
- **Reporting**: Allow users to report inappropriate content and behavior.
- **Blocking**: Enable users to block other users to prevent unwanted interactions.
- **Moderation Tools**: Provide tools for moderators to manage the community and enforce rules.

## 7. **Community Engagement**
- **Polls and Surveys**: Allow users to create and participate in polls and surveys.
- **Contests**: Enable users to participate in contests and win prizes.
- **Rewards**: Provide rewards for active and engaged users.

## 8. **Community Analytics**
- **User Activity**: Track user activity to identify trends and patterns.
- **Engagement Metrics**: Measure engagement metrics like likes, comments, and shares.
- **Community Growth**: Monitor community growth and identify areas for improvement.
